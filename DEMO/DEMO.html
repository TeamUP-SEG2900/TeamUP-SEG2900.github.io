How to Use the Interactive Map

1. Select a Park

Click on any **red dot** on the map to select a park. This will open a menu with details about that park.

2. View Pickup Games

After selecting a park, you’ll see a list of **available pickup games** happening at that location.

3. Join a Game

Click on the sport you want to play to **join the game**.

  • You will then be able to see your team
  • and also your opponents

You're all set to play!
