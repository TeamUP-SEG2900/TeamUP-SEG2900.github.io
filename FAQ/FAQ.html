Frequently Asked Questions

Do you need to pay for TEAMUP?

You don't need to pay, however the premium version does have more features and helps support us. CLICK HERE TO LEARN MORE

If I'm new to a sport, do I have to play against people who are more experienced?

No! We ask for a general skill level for each sport you play and will give you teammates similar in skill.

If I like my teammates, is there a way that I can continue playing with them in the future?

Yes! After sessions, you can create your own group chat and play together in future games.

What if one of my teammates back out last minute?

Just go to the app and say that a teammate is missing and we'll prioritze filling in the vacant spot
