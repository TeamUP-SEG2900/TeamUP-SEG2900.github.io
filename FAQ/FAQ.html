Frequently Asked Questions

Do you need to pay for TEAMUP?

No! It's completely free. However we do have a premium version of the app

If im new to a sport, do I have to play against people who are more experienced?

No! We ask for a general skill level for each sport you play and will give you teamates similar in skill. Think of it as Skill based matchmaking

If I like my teamates, is there a way that I can continue playing with them in the future?

Yes! After sessions, you can create your own group with it's own chat to play together in future games.

What if one of my teamates back out last minute?

Just go to the app and say that a teamate is missing and we'll prioritze filling in the vacant spot
