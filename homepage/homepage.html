TEAMUP!

Play Locale ➡️

Overview

TeamUP! is the ultimate app for organizing and joining pickup sports games. Perfect for anyone interested in spontaneous games like:

  • Basketball
  • Soccer
  • Touch/Flag Football
  • Tennis
  • Pickleball
  • Volleyball

Key Features

  • Quickly find parks for games
  • Create game announcements
  • Vote on participation
  • Connect with players at your skill level
  • Vote for MVP after games

Our Mission

At TeamUP!, we believe in the power of sports to unite people. We aim to create opportunities for connection and collaboration through athletic participation.

Join us in fostering a sense of belonging and promoting an active lifestyle!
